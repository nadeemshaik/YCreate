About Y!Create

Marissa Mayer, CEO of Yahoo!, recently announced banning work from home..a reason being the fact that employees can collaborate and discuss better, if they are together. Well now she can take her decision back. We are here with Y!Create (why not create), an online white board app where users can create and join discussion rooms and discuss stuff over the network from their homes.They can draw ideas, diagrams, charts etc. on a common drawing board, view-able and editable by everyone in real time. There is also a chat application for them to discuss their ideas.

There is a wide range of use for our hack. For example, it can be used in a school environment for teaching, where the students can interact with the teacher through the board/chat. Friends can study together. It may also be used to play simple games (tic-tac-toe!). Who will use it: Business professionals, Teachers, Students, Online businesses, Creative Teams
